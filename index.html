Rules

Hate Speech Isn’t Allowed

Anyone who uses this server to spread hate speech by any means will be removed from the server. Don’t make threatening statements toward a group of people based on their lifestyle, their race, their gender, their sexual orientation/identity, etc. Making ‘jokes’ that fall under this category isn’t allowed either. You’re not exempt from making dark/grim jokes that belittle tragic events within history.

Provocative/Rude Behavior Isn’t Allowed

Don’t bring personal issues within this Discord server. You’ll get punished if you’re involved in any kind of conflict with another server member. This also includes mentioning spicy topics such as politics, bold beliefs, etc. A staff member will give you a chance to drop the topic, failing to comply will result in punishments.

Spamming/Advertising Isn’t Allowed

Flooding the text channels with advertisements/nonsense isn’t allowed. It hinders people from having a proper discussion. Advertising isn’t allowed, period. Advertising via people’s direct messages will result in a ban. Not a warning, not a mute, but a ban. Chaining is considered spamming too.

Doxxing Isn’t Allowed

We take people’s privacy seriously, if you’re caught revealing personal info, you will get banned. It goes against Discord’s TOS. Asking for personal information such as age, full name, address, and etc may result in a harsh punishment. Staff members are only allowed to ask people for their age if they’re suspicious that they’re underaged.

False Information & Mini Modding

You’re not allowed to mini mod. Mini modding could mean telling users to not break the rules. Acting like a moderator instead of alerting a moderator causes more harm than good. You’re not allowed to spread false information around with malicious intentions in mind. Scamming, fear-mongering, and gossiping fall under this category.

Impersonation

You’re not allowed to impersonate influencers, staff members, server members, Discord bots, and etc.

Keep it Appropriate

This isn’t an e dating server. You’re not allowed to express romantic/sexual feelings toward anyone within this server. Don’t make references to shock content, adult websites, inappropriate terms, and etc. Directing to anything NSFW will result in a ban. If you post NSFW content, you’ll get banned. We don’t tolerate NSFW content at all.

Follow Discord’s TOS & Guidelines

Click here to view Discord’s guidelines
As the TOS states, you must be 13 years or older in order to use Discord. Under any circumstances, don’t lie/joke about your age.

FAQ

How do I become a staff member?

If you want to become a staff member, you must apply through our moderator application. Moderator applications are usually closed. We open mod applications when we need more staff, and it’ll usually require Lvl 10+ to view the channel, though it is likely to change with time.

How can I get help from a staff member?

You can DM any active staff member to report a person, or ask any questions or clarification on anything.

Why did my nickname get moderated/How do I change my nick?

If your nickname was moderated that means it is inappropriate/violates discord’s TOS. You'll need Lvl 10+ to change your nickname, though if your nickname was moderated you can ask a staff member to change your nickname for you.

How do I send images in chat?

You need to have Lvl 10+ or Nitro Booster to send images and you unlock gif perms at level 15+ or if you’re a nitro booster.

How can I get [x] role?

See #role-info for more detailed/ descriptive information on each of the roles, it shows how any role in the server can be obtained.

How do I check my rank/level up?

You can receive XP to level up by sending a message every minute in the server, use !rank in #bot-commands to check your level. You can also check the server's leaderboards by doing !levels.

Why can’t I post links?

Typically, links sent by people are malicious and people can accidentally click on them while in chat. To minimize this and keep all of our users safe, links are blocked from being sent in the server, this includes ones used in the Discord GIF picker unless you have gif perms.

Can I speak other languages in this server?

No, you can only speak English in this server. This is because most of the staff team speaks predominantly English, because of this reason we require you to speak English for moderation purposes.

How can I suggest new features for the server?

You may suggest things in #suggestions or you could message a staff member and discuss it with them, if they believe it's a good idea they can converse with other staff members and talk about implementing it.

Can I use alt accounts?

Everyone is allowed to have alts in the server, but you can't use them to enter multiple times in giveaways, or to bypass any sort of punishment another account received.

How do I report a Staff member?

If you have issues with a specific staff member or a punishment you've received, please DM a head moderator or above to report. Please be aware any false or forged evidence in an attempt to get a moderator fired will be handled promptly. In most cases resulting in a ban.

If you have any questions or concerns feel free to DM an active staff member.
